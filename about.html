About The Islamic Center of Henrico & Masjid Al-Falah

Welcome to the Islamic Center of Henrico & Masjid Al-Falah, a vibrant community center dedicated to serving Muslims in Richmond, Virginia and the surrounding areas.

Our Vision

At Masjid Al-Falah, we strive to create a vibrant and inclusive community that provides spiritual guidance, education, and support to Muslims of all ages and backgrounds.

Our Services

Daily Prayers

We offer all five daily prayers (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha) in congregation, providing a sacred space for worship and spiritual connection.

Quranic Studies

Classes for children and adults focusing on Quran recitation, memorization, and understanding its meanings and teachings.

Islamic Education

Regular classes and workshops on various aspects of Islamic knowledge including fiqh, hadith, seerah, and contemporary issues.

Join Us

We invite you to become a part of our community. Whether you are new to the area, visiting Richmond, or simply interested in learning more about Islam, you will find a warm welcome at our center.
